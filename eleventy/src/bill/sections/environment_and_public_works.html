Title VI: Environment and Public Works

This title of H.R. 1 makes significant changes to programs related to the environment and public works. The changes are far-reaching, affecting everything from clean air and water to environmental justice. Below is a detailed breakdown of the key provisions in this title.

Rescission of Funding for Clean Heavy-Duty Vehicles

This section of the bill rescinds funding for clean heavy-duty vehicles.

  • Rescission of Funds: The bill rescinds all unobligated balances of amounts made available for clean heavy-duty vehicles.

Repeal of Greenhouse Gas Reduction Fund

This section of the bill repeals the Greenhouse Gas Reduction Fund.

  • Repeal: The bill repeals the Greenhouse Gas Reduction Fund, which was created by the Inflation Reduction Act to provide funding for projects that reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts

This section of the bill provides funding for the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts.

  • Appropriations: The bill appropriates $256,657,000 for the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts for fiscal year 2025.

Project Sponsor Opt-In Fees for Environmental Reviews

This section of the bill allows project sponsors to pay a fee to expedite environmental reviews.

  • Opt-In Fees: The bill allows project sponsors to pay a fee to the Council on Environmental Quality to expedite environmental reviews.
