Title V: Energy and Natural Resources

This title of H.R. 1 makes significant changes to programs related to energy and natural resources. The changes are far-reaching, affecting everything from oil and gas leasing to the Strategic Petroleum Reserve. Below is a detailed breakdown of the key provisions in this title.

Subtitle A: Oil and Gas Leasing

This subtitle focuses on changes to oil and gas leasing on federal lands.

  • Onshore Oil and Gas Leasing: The bill repeals the increase in royalty rates for onshore oil and gas leases that was included in the Inflation Reduction Act. It also mandates the immediate resumption of quarterly oil and gas lease sales on federal lands.
  • Offshore Oil and Gas Leasing: The bill mandates a minimum of 30 new offshore oil and gas lease sales in the Gulf of Mexico and 6 new offshore lease sales in the Alaska region.
  • Royalties on Extracted Methane: The bill repeals the royalties on extracted methane that were included in the Inflation Reduction Act.
  • Alaska Oil and Gas Leasing: The bill mandates new oil and gas lease sales in the Alaska region.
  • National Petroleum Reserve-Alaska: The bill restores the oil and gas leasing program in the National Petroleum Reserve-Alaska.

Subtitle B: Mining

This subtitle focuses on changes to mining on federal lands.

  • Coal Leasing: The bill requires the Secretary of the Interior to immediately resume coal leasing on federal lands.
  • Coal Royalty: The bill reduces the royalty rate for coal mined on federal lands.
  • Leases for Known Recoverable Coal Resources: The bill makes additional lands available for coal leasing.
  • Authorization to Mine Federal Coal: The bill authorizes the mining of federal coal in certain circumstances.

Subtitle C: Lands

This subtitle focuses on changes to the management of federal lands.

  • Timber Sales and Long-Term Contracting: The bill increases the amount of timber that can be sold from national forests and other federal lands.
  • Renewable Energy Fees on Federal Land: The bill introduces new fees for renewable energy projects on federal lands.
  • Renewable Energy Revenue Sharing: The bill changes the revenue-sharing agreements with states for renewable energy projects on federal lands.
  • Rescission of National Park Service and Bureau of Land Management Funds: The bill rescinds funding for the National Park Service and the Bureau of Land Management.
  • Celebrating America's 250th Anniversary: The bill provides funding for the celebration of the 250th anniversary of the founding of the United States.

Subtitle D: Energy

This subtitle focuses on changes to energy policy.

  • Strategic Petroleum Reserve: The bill provides funding to maintain and replenish the Strategic Petroleum Reserve.
  • Repeals; Rescissions: The bill repeals a number of energy-related provisions from the Inflation Reduction Act.
  • Energy Dominance Financing: The bill provides funding for energy dominance financing.
  • Transformational Artificial Intelligence Models: The bill provides funding for the development of transformational artificial intelligence models for the energy sector.

Subtitle E: Water

This subtitle focuses on changes to water policy.

  • Water Conveyance and Surface Water Storage Enhancement: The bill provides funding for water conveyance and surface water storage enhancement projects.
