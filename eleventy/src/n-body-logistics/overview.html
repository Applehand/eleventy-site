N-body logistics

A 2D orbital mechanics, logistics optimization, factory automation, and business management game.

As the chief corporate officer of an interplanetary extraction/manufacturing/logistics/security conglomerate, your job is to ensure smooth inter-divisional operations while reporting outcomes to shareholders including corporate investors, involved governments, and the company workforce.

  • Design advanced equipment.
  • Maneuver orbital trajectories.
  • Research innovative technologies.
  • Extract discovered resources.
  • Construct manufacturing facilities.
  • Establish logistics routes.
  • Oversee staffing decisions.
  • Outflank the competition.
  • Manage corporate politics.
  • Allocate company resources.
  • Deliver quality products.
  • Ensure operational security.
  • Gather critical intelligence.
  • Complete lucrative contracts.
  • Increase company reputation.
  • Real time with 1x/2x/3x speed.
  • Lots of menus and UI.
  • Event popups.
  • Develop characters through promotions and experience, allowing success stories like a line worker moving up to department CEO.
  • Long-term play-through legacies like company quota milestones, employees of the month, yearly corporate awards by department, high-profile retirements, disaster memorials, and company strikes.
  • The most notable events/characters from past campaigns are added to the history of a new campaign as corporate lore or added into the pool of possible employees to hire.

Inspired by Satisfactor/y/io, Victoria 3, Oxygen Not Included.
